About Our Firm

Founded in 1990 by Adv. A.V. Linpane, our firm has grown to become one of the most respected legal practices in the Nagpur High Court region. We combine traditional values with modern legal approaches to deliver outstanding results for our clients.

Our office, strategically located in the heart of Nagpur's legal district, is designed to provide a comfortable and confidential environment for consultations. We understand that legal matters can be stressful, and we strive to make the process as smooth as possible for our clients.

With over 1000+ cases successfully resolved and a client satisfaction rate of 98%, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and our track record of success across various legal domains in Maharashtra.
