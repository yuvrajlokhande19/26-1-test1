About Our Firm

Established in 1990 by Adv. A.V. Linpane, Linpane Legal Associates has grown to become one of Nagpur's most respected and trusted law firms. With our principal office strategically located in Nagpur, we are perfectly positioned to handle cases across Maharashtra, with special expertise in Nagpur High Court proceedings.

Our firm combines traditional legal values with modern approaches to deliver outstanding results for our clients. We understand that legal matters can be stressful and complex, which is why we provide personalized attention and clear communication throughout the legal process. Our office in Nagpur is designed to provide a comfortable, confidential environment for consultations.

We have successfully represented clients in numerous landmark cases in Nagpur High Court and various district courts across Maharashtra. Our expertise spans across criminal defense, civil litigation, family law matters, corporate disputes, banking litigation, and comprehensive notary services. We are committed to providing accessible justice and have helped thousands of clients navigate the Indian legal system.

At Linpane Legal Associates, we believe in building long-term relationships with our clients based on trust, transparency, and results. Our team stays updated with the latest legal developments and court judgments to provide you with the most current and effective legal advice.
