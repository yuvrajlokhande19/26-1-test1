About Our Firm

Founded in 1990 by Adv. Ashish V. Linpane, our firm has grown to become one of the most respected legal practices in Nagpur and Wardha region. We combine traditional values with modern legal approaches to deliver outstanding results for our clients across Maharashtra.

Our office in Nagpur is strategically located near the District Court and High Court, designed to provide a comfortable and confidential environment for consultations. We understand that legal matters can be stressful, and we strive to make the process as smooth as possible for our clients.

With a team of 7 specialized advocates, we handle cases ranging from local disputes to complex High Court and Supreme Court matters. Our success rate of 95% and client satisfaction of 98% speaks volumes about our dedication and expertise.
