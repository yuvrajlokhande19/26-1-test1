About Our Firm

Founded in 1990 by Adv. A.V. Linpane, our Nagpur-based firm has grown to become one of the most respected legal practices in Maharashtra. With our main office in Nagpur and branch in Wardha, we combine traditional values with modern legal approaches to deliver outstanding results for our clients across Vidarbha region.

Our office is strategically located in Nagpur's legal district, designed to provide a comfortable and confidential environment for consultations. We understand that legal matters can be stressful, and we strive to make the process as smooth as possible for our clients from Nagpur, Wardha, Amravati, Akola, and surrounding districts.

With over 1000+ cases successfully resolved in Nagpur High Court and District Courts, and a client satisfaction rate of 98%, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and our track record of success across various legal domains throughout Maharashtra.
